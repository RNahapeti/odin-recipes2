Chocolate Dipped Pumpkin Pie Bites

Description

Love pumpkin pie? Crave chocolate? This quick and easy treat from Trader Joe's should satisfy all your cravings!

Make a wonderful presentation by half-dipping the pie bites (as seen in the image above), or customize them with your favorite crunchy garnish. I personally prefer to sprinkle some crushed Werther's Original caramel hard candies as a magical burst of flavor!

Ingredients

  • Â½ bar Trader Joe's Pound Plus chocolate (semi-sweet or dark)
  • 1 whole Trader Joe's Pumpkin Pie
  • Crunchy garnish of choice (optional)
    Examples:
    • Sea salt
    • Sliced almonds
    • Sunflower seeds
    • Crushed Werther's Caramels (my fav!)

Steps

  1. Place Trader Joe's Pumpkin Pie in freezer for 1 hour. This helps with making cleanly cut pie bites.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  3. Melt chocolate in microwave, stirring in between until smooth.
  4. Remove pie from freezer and use sharp knife to slice pie into 1 inch cubes.
  5. Place bites on fork 1 by 1 and dip into melted chocolate. If fully covering, use spoon to pour more chocolate over the bite. Allow excess chocolate to drop before placing onto parchment.
  6. (Optional) Garnish bites with crunchy toppings
  7. Serve once hardened - bone apple teeth!
